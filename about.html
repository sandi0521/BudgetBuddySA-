🎯 Objective

The objective of BudgetBuddy SA is to help students develop good financial habits by providing a simple and user-friendly budgeting platform. The system enables users to record their income and expenses, monitor their remaining balance, generate budget reports, receive smart budgeting advice, and make informed financial decisions.

👨‍💻 Developer

BudgetBuddy SA was designed and developed by Sandisiwe Miya, a Diploma in Information Technology student. The project was created to help students manage their finances through an easy-to-use budgeting application while showcasing practical web development skills.
